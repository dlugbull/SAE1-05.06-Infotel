Economic Overview

Infotel is a French digital services company (ESN) founded in 1979, specializing in the digital transformation of major European corporations. Listed on Euronext Paris since 1999, the company holds a strong position in the French ESN market, ranking 31st nationally.

Sector Analysis

Infotel focuses a significant share of its business in the Banking/Finance sector (39.2%), followed by Industry (26.3%) and Services/Transport (19.1%). This distribution reflects a strong dependence on financial sectors, although diversification into industry and services mitigates this risk.

The Insurance sector accounts for 11.4% of revenue, while Public Administration represents a more modest share at 4.1%. This sectoral diversification enables Infotel to benefit from multiple revenue sources, strengthening its resilience to sector-specific economic fluctuations.
