Infotel's history

Infotel is a company created on 31 December 1979 by Michel Koutchouk, Bernard Lafforet, Josyane Muller and Alain Hallereau. She works in the field of databases, that is to say systems that allow storing and organizing large amounts of information. Infotel develops software, that is to say computer programs, some work in real time, which means they process data immediately, without delay. The company legally separates its service activities (helping customers) and software publishing (creating programs). Thanks to the success of its software, Infotel opens a subsidiary in the United States.

In 1989, Infotel opens an agency in Toulouse. In 1990, it creates a company in Germany called Infotel GmbH. In 1998, the company experienced great growth (significant increase in its activities and revenues) and obtained ISO 9001 certification, which is an international standard proving that their organization meets quality standards.

On 21 January 1999, Infotel went public in Paris, that is to say it began selling shares to the public. That year, it signs a partnership with IBM, a large computer group, to develop software around DB2, a database management system marketed by IBM. Infotel adopts a strategy called "Web-To-Database", which means offering complete technical solutions to help companies connect their websites to their databases.

Infotel is expanding its offering with the creation of Archive Data Software, a company specializing in archiving software like Arcsys, which allows for the secure storage and management of old data. In June 2011, Infotel bought the German company Insoft Software GmbH, thus strengthening its presence in Europe.

The company is developing in the field of mobility (applications for smartphones and tablets) and supports large companies in the management of Big Data, that is to say the analysis of very large amounts of data.

Infotel focuses on three objectives: innovate (create new technologies or services), have big ambitions (develop further) and ensure continuity (maintain its activities and stable growth).

Infotel aims for four axes for its development: Outperform their market (to be better than their competitors), Further develop software creation, Implement an attractive and dynamic HR policy (attract and retain talent), Accelerate the growth of the company in France and internationally.
